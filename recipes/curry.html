Curry

I've been horribly obsessed with curry since I played sword and shield. Credits to Victoria Rosenthal for this recipe and image!


Saffron Rice Ingredients

  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
  • 1/2 onion, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 1/2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3 3/4 cups vegetable broth

Curry Ingredients

  • 8 white potatoes, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 3 carrots, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 8 baby portabello mushrooms, quartered
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 2 tbsp ginger paste
  • 2 tbsp garlic paste
  • 3 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp coriander, ground
  • 1 tsp cumin, ground
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon, ground
  • 1 tbsp cardamom, ground
  • 2 tsp turmeric, ground
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1/3 cup plain yogurt
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

Saffron Rice

  1. Place the saffron and boiling water in a cup and allow to steep for 5 minutes. Heat a saucepan with butter over medium heat. Melt the butter and add the onions and cook until translucent.
  2. Add the rice and cook until slightly toasted, about 3 minutes. Add the saffron water, salt, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to low. Cover and cook until the rice has cooked, about 20 minutes.

Curry

  1. Preheat your oven to 425Â° Bring a pot of water to a boil and add the potatoes and carrots. Cook for 8 minutes. Transfer the potatoes and carrots to a bowl with mushrooms and olive oil.
  2. Place the oiled vegetables on a baking sheet and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake for 20 minutes, toss, and bake for another 15 minutes.
  3. While the vegetables are roasting, combine the garam masala, cardamom, turmeric, paprika, coriander, cumin, chili powder, and cinnamon in a small bowl. Heat a large pan with butter over medium-high heat. Add the red onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and ginger paste and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes. Add the spice mixture and mix until well combined.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth. Mix well, making sure there are no clumps of spices left. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the yogurt and honey. Taste and season with salt and pepper if needed. Add the roasted vegetables. Serve with saffron rice.
